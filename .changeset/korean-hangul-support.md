Support Korean (Hangul) writing system and add Nanum Pen Script as a built-in font. The bundle ships ~650 most-frequent precomposed Hangul syllables (KS X 1001 common band ∪ top-N Korean-Wikipedia-frequency, capped by the Google Fonts subsetting ceiling) plus the 40 modern compatibility jamo (19 consonants ㄱ–ㅎ + 21 vowels ㅏ–ㅣ) and the Latin baseline. Hangul is precomposed in Unicode, so no shaper is required.
